Balloon pillars add elegance, color and effervescence to a child’s birthday party. BIRTHDAY PLANNERS makes balloon pillars in numerous ways that will be an add-on in your decorations. We use these pillars to add a distinct charm to a party. So if it is your girl's birthday party, we’ll make her and all her friends spellbound with castles having balloon pillar gates. Placing elegant party themed balloon pillars at the entrance would grab everyone’s eyeballs and add classiness to your decorations.

Bright shining balloons when used on a pillar would delight children and placing them around the food-stall tables, entrance, dance-floors and in a multitude of shapes would be the center of attraction for your little ones’ birthday party. We design balloon pillars for all sorts of birthday themes including – carnival, superhero, pirates, Disney World, princess and Halloween to name a few. With the help of fancy ribbons and lovely balloons we transform a normal balloon pillar into the heart and soul of the party. Each balloon pillar is unique, loved by the children and so cute that they’ll want to take them home.
At Birthday Bumps, our aim is to decorate birthday parties in the style that the kids would love and keep them flabbergasted. After the theme is decided, we then design these balloon pillars in a number of ways and based on your choice we use them. With us, you do not have to worry about anything, we are experts in planning, organizing and managing picture perfect parties that are loved by one and all.
